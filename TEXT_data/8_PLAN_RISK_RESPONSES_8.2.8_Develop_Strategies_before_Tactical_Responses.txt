8.2.8 develop strategies before tactical responses risk response planning should be carried out in an open minded manner rather than adopting the first response that seems to be feasible. the responses should be planned at a general strategic level and the strategy validated and agreed upon prior to developing the detailed tactical approach. once the responses have been planned at a strategic level they should be expanded into actions at the tactical level and integrated into the project management plan e.g. schedule budget and resource assignments . this activity may generate additional secondary risks which will need to be addressed at this time.
